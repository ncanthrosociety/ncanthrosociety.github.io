NC Anthro Groups

Blue Ridge Furfare

The Blue Ridge Furfare is a convention located in Asheville, North Carolina. Expect cool mountain weather, chill Asheville vibes, and some unique programming.

Western North Carolina Furs

The Western North Carolina Furs are located in and around Asheville, NC. They try to have a fun activity planned at least once a month!
