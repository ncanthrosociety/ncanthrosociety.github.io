North Carolina Anthropomorphic Society

Hello and greetings from the North Carolina Anthropomorphic Society, Inc. (NCAS). We are a nonprofit organization operating out of Raleigh, North Carolina. Our goal is to organize events that enrich the lives of our members and give back to the anthropomorphic community throughout the state and surrounding region.

We aim to host a variety of events taking place throughout the year, and are always actively exploring additional ways we can contribute to the anthropomorphic community. Check back here, or follow us on social media, to stay up to date on all the latest events. Our founders, staff, and volunteers hope to see you soon!

If you are interested in becoming a part of NCAS, see our staff and volunteer information.
