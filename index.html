North Carolina Anthropomorphic Society

Hello and greetings from the North Carolina Anthropomorphic Society, Inc. (NCAS). We are a nonprofit organization operating out of Raleigh, North Carolina. Our goal is to organize events for our members to enrich their lives and give back to the anthropomorphic community throughout the state and surrounding region.

We are just getting started here at the NCAS HQ, and are well on our way to begin regular operations. Our initial goal for 2020 will be hosting a variety of events taking place throughout the year, and we will be actively exploring additional ways we can contribute to the anthropomorphic community. Stay tuned for more information about our plans and events in the coming months. Our founders, staff, and volunteers are excited to be embarking on this journey with you!

If you are interested in becoming a part of NCAS, or partnering with us, see our contact section.

Our Mission Statement

The North Carolina Anthropomorphic Society works to foster the celebration of anthropomorphic activities and culture by hosting events that engage, entertain, and empower our members to be a positive influence in our communities and beyond. We do this because we believe in the openness of our members, in their passion, in their compassion, and in the friendships they form.
