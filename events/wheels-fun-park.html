FAQ

What is the Wheels Fun Park?

Wheels Fun Park is a roller skating center located in Durham, NC. We have their skating rink and arcade reserved for two hours!

Is Wheels Fun park fursuit friendly?

Yes! They have agreed to let us have fursuits out on the skating rink. We ask that you do not bring tails or other fursuit items that will drag on the floor, because they pose a safety risk to skaters.

Where is the Wheels Fun Park located? How do I get there?

715 N. Hoover Road, Durham, NC 27703.

What do I need to do to prepare or bring to the event?

Bring yourself, your fursuit (optional), and your skates (optional)! Skate rentals are also available at the venue.

Does my registration include skate rental?

Yes!
