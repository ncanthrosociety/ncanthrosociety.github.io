FAQ

Is the Greensboro Science Center fursuit friendly?

Fursuits and other cosplay are not allowed at this event for the safety of the animals and other guests. Thank you for understanding.

How should I prepare for the event?

Bring comfortable clothes and good shoes for walking. The science center is partially outside, so make sure you dress for the weather.

Where are we meeting up?

We will be meeting up just outside the science center entrance near the admissions stand. Look for a sign with the NCAS logo! You can find a map here.
